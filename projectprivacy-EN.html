Privacy Statement

 1. Description of the processing operations.

  This project conducts collects information on respondents’ political preferences and behaviors. Panellists recruited by Cint are offered to participate in the survey. If they accept the invitation, they are forward to the platform Qualtrics where they need to give informed consent before entering the questionnaire. Qualtrics complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation, and will record the answers in a secured a protected database. When the data collection is completed, the data will be transferred to the institutional cloud repository. The data will be fully anonymous and analyzed through statistical procedure. For replication purposes and secondary analyses, the anonymous data will be me available to the scholarly community.

 2. What personal information do we collect, for what purpose(s), and through which technical means?

 The respondents will complete a survey that includes question on political preferences and behaviors (such as left/right placement, political interest, support for hypothetical political regime) and socio-demographic characteristics (such as gender, age, education, occupation, region of residence). The answers to the survey will help us understand how people vote in elections and how they form their opinions on political questions.

 3. Who has access to your information and to whom is it disclosed?

 The survey is conducted by Prof Filip Kostelka and Dr Eva Krejcova who are the only individuals having access to the respondents’ answers recorded in the survey. In the publishing process, the fully anonymized data may be accessed by other academics.

 4. How do we protect and safeguard your information?

 The results of the completed survey will be stored on the Qualtrics platform. The platform does not record any information on the IP address, contact information or location data. Upon completion of the field period, data will be transferred to the European University Institute’s institutional cloud storage platform, where they will be accessible only by Professor Filip Kostelka and Dr Eva Krejcova through a password protected folder. After the transfer to the EUI institutional cloud storage platform, the survey results on Qualtrics will be deleted.

 5. How long do we keep your data?

 Throughout the whole process, the research team guarantees the anonymity of the respondents. The data used for statistical analyses will contain no information that would enable anyone to identify any of the respondents. Such anonymous data will be kept indefinitely for secondary analyses.

 6. How can you verify, modify, or delete your information?

 If you wish to verify which personal data are stored, or you wish to access, correct, or delete your personal data, please contact the Data Controller at filip.kostelka@eui.eu. You will receive a reply within 30 working days of receipt of the request. Remarks concerning this Privacy Statement can be addressed to data_protection_officer@eui.eu.

 7. Right of recourse

 According to Article 16 of the EUI’s Data Protection Policy, survey participants enjoy the following rights concerning their personal data: a) to be informed whether, how, by whom and for which purpose they are processed; b) to ask for their rectification, in case they are inaccurate or incomplete; c) to demand their erasure in case the processing is unlawful or no longer lawful (‘the right to be forgotten’); d) to block their further processing whilst the conditions under points b) and c) above are verified.
